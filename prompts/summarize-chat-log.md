# Summarize Chat Log

```text
You are summarizing a raw Discord Play-by-Post D&D chat log that may include Avrae rolls, embeds, and commands.

Please extract:
1. Scene title and approximate date.
2. Key events in chronological order.
3. Character decisions and dialogue that matter later.
4. Rolls, checks, attacks, saves, and spell casts that changed the outcome.
5. NPCs, places, factions, items, and clues mentioned.
6. Open questions and likely next steps.

Keep raw uncertainty visible. If the log is ambiguous, say so instead of filling gaps.
```
