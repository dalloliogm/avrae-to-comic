# Catch-Up Prompt

Use this after pasting recent chat logs into the campaign.

```text
You are helping me catch up on a Play-by-Post D&D campaign played on Discord with Avrae.

Use the campaign notes, character notes, chat logs, and session summaries provided in this folder.

Please produce:
1. A concise recap of what happened.
2. What my character knows.
3. What my character likely wants next.
4. Important unresolved questions.
5. Three possible in-character replies I could post, with different tones.

Do not invent facts that are not supported by the notes. Mark uncertainty clearly.
```
