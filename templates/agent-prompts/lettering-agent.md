Use the current page script and rendered/base-art dimensions. Place captions and balloons so they do not cover faces, key action, important props, or panel transitions. Prefer editing lettering over regenerating art when only wording or placement is wrong.

Keep text concise. Do not invent new story facts. Do not edit other workers' files. You are not alone in the codebase; preserve existing edits and report the exact path you changed.
