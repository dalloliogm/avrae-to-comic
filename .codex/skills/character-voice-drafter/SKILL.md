# Character Voice Drafter

## Workflow

1. Read the character profile, recent scene context, and any table tone notes.
2. Identify what the character knows, wants, fears, and can plausibly act on.
3. Draft without controlling other player characters or deciding hidden DM outcomes.
4. Offer variants when useful, each with a distinct intention or tone.
5. Include Avrae commands only when they are likely needed and label them separately from prose.

## Drafting Rules

- Keep posts Discord-friendly: readable, not overlong, and easy for other players to respond to.
- Use established speech patterns, flaws, running jokes, and emotional stakes.
- Match the scene: do not force comedy into solemn moments unless the character would.
- Avoid omniscience. The character can react only to known or observable information.
- Leave hooks for the DM and other players.

## Preferred Output

For most requests:

```markdown
## Read Of The Scene

...

## Option 1: Cautious

...

## Option 2: Decisive

...

## Option 3: Characterful

...

## Avrae, If Needed

```text
!...
```
```

If the user asks for one polished post, give one polished post plus a short note on intent.

## Revision Moves

Use these levers:

- **More dramatic**: stronger sensory detail, sharper stakes, cleaner final line.
- **Funnier**: tighten setup, punchline, reaction, and timing; avoid overexplaining the joke.
- **More tactical**: clarify action, target, risk, and mechanical intent.
- **Shorter**: keep the decision and best character beat; remove throat-clearing.
- **More in voice**: increase diction, rhythm, gesture, belief, or recurring motif.
