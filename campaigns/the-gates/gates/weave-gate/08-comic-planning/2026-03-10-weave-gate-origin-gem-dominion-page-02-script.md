# Weave Gate Origin Gem Dominion Comic Script: Page 2

## Page Goal

Reset from the Page 1 climax into the mission's strange, dryly comic opening. This page should explain how Don gets pulled into the mission while preserving the mystery of the Origin Gems and the danger at Weave Gate. Use caption boxes more actively than Page 1: this page is a narrated recap that guides the reader through the setup.

## Layout

- Six panels.
- Panel 1 is a quiet domestic contrast after the cold open.
- Panel 2 introduces the portal and bell.
- Panel 3 shows the note and Skimmer warning.
- Panel 4 is a wide void-transit panel.
- Panels 5 and 6 establish Alivia's study and the mission urgency.

## Panel 1

Warm, relaxed scene. Don and Dan are in semi-retirement mode: an old green orc wizard and his icy-blue simulacrum counterpart, relaxed with food, drink, and a hint of magical comfort around them. Don's hat and staff should be nearby but not battle-ready. Dan should be clearly icy and blue, not just Don in alternate colors.

The page should feel almost too comfortable after Page 1.

**Caption:** Earlier that day, an orc wizard and his simulacrum were busy devouring a banquet.

**Art Notes:** Do not over-explain the retirement setup. One image of comfort is enough: plates, goblets, cushions, or spellbook clutter.

**Prompt Note:** Don Explodicus, old green orc wizard with white beard and conical hat, relaxing with icy blue simulacrum Dan, fantasy semi-retirement scene with food and magical clutter, warm comic panel.

## Panel 2

A tiny polite bell rings behind Don. The portal opens like a vertical mouth into dark void, with a small domestic bell absurdly attached to its edge. Don turns halfway, eyebrows raised. Dan leans or looks toward it with calm curiosity.

**Caption:** Retirement, as usual, had other plans.

**SFX:** ding

**Don:** That is either very polite or very ominous.

**Art Notes:** This is the tonal bridge from Page 1's hard cut. Keep the portal visually small and strange, not yet epic.

**Prompt Note:** Small magical portal with a tiny doorbell appears behind an old orc wizard and icy simulacrum, warm room, dry fantasy comedy, comic art.

## Panel 3

Close-up on the portal mouth. Inside the dark void floats a small circular gray stone platform. A note rests on it, lit by the portal glow.

**Caption:** The invitation was short, urgent, and exactly as reassuring as a floating stone in a void could be.

**Note Text:** Urgent Matter. Weave Gate. Come at once.

**Note Text, smaller:** Don't step off the Skimmer.

**Dan:** Skimmer?

**Don:** That sounds like a word invented by someone who expects us to trust them.

**Art Notes:** The note text must be readable. Make the void behind it visually clean.

**Prompt Note:** Close-up of a small floating gray stone platform inside a dark magical portal, handwritten note reading urgent matter Weave Gate come at once, don't step off the Skimmer, fantasy comic panel.

## Panel 4

Wide panel. Don and Dan stand on the Skimmer as it travels through starless void. The platform has expanded just enough for them and their gear, but it has no railings. The portal they entered from is now behind them; another portal rushes toward them ahead.

Don holds himself steady with forced dignity. Dan, being made of ice, looks more composed but skeptical.

**Caption:** The Skimmer did, technically, skim.

**Don:** No rails. Very wizardly.

**Dan:** Efficient, though.

**Art Notes:** This should show why the warning mattered without making the Skimmer look unsafe in practice. It is eerie, smooth, and too convenient.

**Prompt Note:** Two orc wizards, one green and one icy blue simulacrum, ride a small floating stone platform through a starless void toward a glowing portal, no railings, fantasy comic wide panel.

## Panel 5

Wide reveal of Alivia's study. The room is a wizard's wonderland: glowing vials and pipes, crystal balls, animated quill, self-turning book, and a large carved table full of arcane energy. Alivia stands in ornate Robe of the Archmagi with gems at fingers, neck, and ears. Brother Bjorn stands beside her in plain faded white monk robes with a quarterstaff and pouch.

On the table are two visible Origin Gems: one blue, one yellow. They should feel important and strange.

**Caption:** Weave Gate.

**Caption:** The crisis began in a wizard's wonderland, which is often the least comforting kind.

**Art Notes:** Alivia should read as controlled but worried. Bjorn should look plain, nervous, and out of place in the ornate study.

**Prompt Note:** Ornate wizard study filled with glowing vials, crystal balls, animated quill, carved arcane table, worried elven archmage in jeweled robe and plain nervous monk beside blue and yellow Origin Gems, fantasy comic wide panel.

## Panel 6

Medium panel on Alivia and Bjorn at the table. Alivia gestures toward the Origin Gems while Bjorn looks uncomfortable. Don and Dan are foreground silhouettes, listening.

**Caption:** Alivia, Keeper of Weave Gate, had found a problem old enough to predate almost everything.

**Alivia:** I am Alivia, Keeper of Weave Gate. These are Origin Gems: relics containing the essence of magic from the dawn of the Planes.

**Bjorn:** A former Brother of mine found one. Dormo. He became obsessed with the Negative Energy Plane.

**Art Notes:** Keep exposition compressed. The page should end with the sense that the mission is serious, but Page 3 will deliver the visual threat.

**Prompt Note:** Elven archmage explains glowing Origin Gems to two orc wizards while nervous monk stands nearby, fantasy comic panel, urgent briefing.

## Lettering Notes

- The note text should be hand-lettered or visually distinct from dialogue.
- Don and Dan's dialogue can be light, but keep it sparse.
- Use a small caption for "Weave Gate" rather than a large title card.

## Continuity Checks

- The portal has a small bell attached.
- The note includes the Skimmer warning.
- The Skimmer expands to carry Don, Dan, and gear.
- Alivia is a former Defender and current Keeper of Weave Gate.
- Bjorn is plain-robed and nervous; his order remains deliberately vague.
- The visible Origin Gems in the study are blue and yellow.
