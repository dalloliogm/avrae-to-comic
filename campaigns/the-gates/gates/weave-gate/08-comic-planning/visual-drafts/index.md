## Direction

Use rough thumbnail pages for first-pass layout review. Generate future base art without captions, dialogue, or SFX, leaving blank space for lettering. Add text afterward with [letter_comic_page.py](../../../../../../../scripts/letter_comic_page.py) so wording can change without regenerating art.

Suggested generation instruction:

> No text, no captions, no speech bubbles, no SFX. Leave clear blank areas where captions and balloons can be added later.
