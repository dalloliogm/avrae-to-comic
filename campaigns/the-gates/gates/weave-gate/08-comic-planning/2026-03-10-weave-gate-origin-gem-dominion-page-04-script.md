# Weave Gate Origin Gem Dominion Comic Script: Page 4

## Page Goal

Show Don and Dan acting like veteran high-level spellcasters before Dominion changes the rules. This page should feel powerful and competent, so the next page's antimagic collapse has weight.

Use recap captions to explain the tactical beats without requiring the reader to understand D&D mechanics.

## Layout

- Five panels.
- Panel 1 shows Dan entering dragon form and stripping Dominion's protections.
- Panel 2 shows Don preparing Sunburst.
- Panel 3 shows the radiant blast hitting acolytes and washing over Dominion.
- Panel 4 shows Dominion insulted and gathering Arcane Cataclysm.
- Panel 5 ends with the antimagic eruption beginning.

## Panel 1

Wide action panel. Dan, still recognizably an icy orc duplicate of Don, takes on the shape or aura of an ancient topaz dragon while remaining visually tied to Don's blue-white frost identity. He tears glowing ward symbols off Dominion with a sweeping dispel gesture.

**Caption:** Dan moved first, wearing the shape of a dragon and the face of Don carved from ice.

**Caption:** Dominion's protections unraveled.

**Art Notes:** Dan must not become a generic dragon. Keep his icy orc identity visible through face, silhouette, or a ghosted duplicate form.

**Prompt Note:** Rough comic thumbnail, icy blue orc simulacrum of old orc wizard with dragon aura stripping magical wards from towering black-red flaming skull tyrant, simple grayscale with blue magic accents.

## Panel 2

Medium panel on Don. He plants his feet in the ruined clearing, old orc face focused, white beard moving in the charged air. Radiant energy builds between his hands and around his Crown of Stars.

**Caption:** Don answered with sunlight.

**Don:** Let's see how dramatic you look blind.

**Art Notes:** Don can be dryly confident here. This is before he realizes magic will not stay reliable.

**Prompt Note:** Rough comic panel of old green orc wizard with tusks, white beard, conical hat, gathering radiant sunburst magic in ancient ruins.

## Panel 3

Large bright panel. Sunburst detonates across the battlefield: acolytes reel and cover their eyes; Dominion remains standing, battered but not broken. The blast should be the first major light moment of the issue.

**Caption:** The acolytes faltered. The tyrant did not.

**SFX:** WHUMM

**Art Notes:** Use simple silhouettes and a white/yellow burst. Keep Dominion readable through the light.

**Prompt Note:** Rough comic panel, radiant sunburst explosion in ancient forest ruins, acolytes blinded, towering flaming skull armored tyrant still standing, simple high contrast thumbnail.

## Panel 4

Close or medium panel on Dominion. It looks insulted by the audacity of mortal mages. Three arcane orbs spin above its flaming skull, gathering impossible force.

**Caption:** Dominion did not appreciate being treated like a spell problem.

**Dominion:** There is only one master of mystic arts.

**Art Notes:** Dominion's black speech balloon should feel invasive. The three orbs should be very clear.

**Prompt Note:** Rough comic panel, black-red armored flaming skull void lord enraged, three arcane orbs spinning above head, black telepathic speech balloon.

## Panel 5

Full-width bottom panel. The three orbs burst outward into overlapping shockwaves across the ruins. Around the Defenders, magical effects crack, fade, and drop into dead gray circles. Don and Dan are caught in the blast, suddenly small against the effect.

**Caption:** Then Dominion made the battlefield forget magic.

**SFX:** KRA-THOOM

**Art Notes:** This is the bridge into Page 5. It should not fully explain antimagic yet, but it must show the world changing.

**Prompt Note:** Rough comic bottom panel, arcane cataclysm shockwaves spreading across ancient ruins, magic effects cracking and fading into gray antimagic circles, old orc wizard and icy orc simulacrum caught in blast.

## Lettering Notes

- Keep captions clear and explanatory.
- Dominion uses black speech balloons with pale lettering.
- Don's one line should be readable but not dominate the page.

## Continuity Checks

- Dan acts before Don and removes Dominion's Foresight and Shield.
- Dan remains an icy orc simulacrum of Don even when associated with dragon magic.
- Don casts Sunburst, blinding the acolytes.
- Dominion survives and responds with Arcane Cataclysm.
- Page 4 ends as antimagic fields begin to form.
