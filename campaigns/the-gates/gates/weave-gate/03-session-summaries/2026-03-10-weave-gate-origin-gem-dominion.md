## Summary

Defenders are summoned by a polite bell-marked portal and transported via a floating stone "Skimmer" to Weave Gate. Alivia, former Defender and current Keeper of Weave Gate, explains that Origin Gems contain the essence of magic from the dawn of the Planes. Brother Bjorn says Dormo, a fallen former Brother from his order, has become obsessed with the Negative Energy Plane and is likely using a lost Origin Gem in an ancient sanctum.

The Defenders arrive in an overgrown ruin just as Dormo completes a ritual. A void entity called Dominion steps through a tear in reality, wearing black and red armor and wielding a black staff. Tobias immediately kills Dormo, but Dominion proves far more dangerous: Dan dispels Dominion's Foresight and Shield, Don blinds the acolytes with Sunburst, and Dominion responds with Arcane Cataclysm, creating antimagic fields that suppress many summons and magical effects.

The fight becomes a battle to drag Dominion out of its antimagic protection. Riknar grapples it first; Morana traps it with Forcecage; an acolyte rescues it with Plane Shift; Dominion returns with Gate; Tobias and Alistaire keep pressure on the field. When magic fails inside the antimagic zone, Don drops his staff and physically grapples Dominion, drawing on old trauma and stubborn strength to drag it into reach. Morana counters Dominion's attempted Power Word Kill, and Alistaire finishes Dominion with a thunderous Meteor Swarm.

After the battle, Alivia and Bjorn arrive. Bjorn retrieves Dormo's amulet and thanks the Defenders, insisting on a reward. Don cleans himself up, retrieves his staff, and lingers in hope that his simulacrum Dan will return.
