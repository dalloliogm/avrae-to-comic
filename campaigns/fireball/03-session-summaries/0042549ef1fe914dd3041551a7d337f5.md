- Doom: *"Sad that he has to destroy such magnificent machines"* — then crits anyway

**Comic potential:** High for a two-page fight spread. Strong visual contrast (tiny dwarf + flying dhampir + elf rogue vs two gargantuan crystal-eating machines). The competitive kill-stealing adds personality without slowing the action.
