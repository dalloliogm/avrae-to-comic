## Highlighted Scene 1: Pidd vs Giant Crabs

**Setup:** 3'1" kobold rogue Pidd is grabbed by a Giant Crab. Two other crabs join. One player narrates all three crabs and Pidd simultaneously.

**Beats:**
- GC2 grabs Pidd. Pidd (immediately): *"AAAA! AAA! BIG CRABS!! HELP!"*
- Pidd breaks free. GC3 grabs her again.
- Throm punches the crab shell; his hands hurt. *"His attempts at attacking it bounces right off and kind of hurt his hands"*
- GC2 checks on GC3 grabbing Pidd: *"YOU DON'T SOUND LIKE YOU'RE HURT ARE YOU OKAY!?!!?*" — addressed to the crab
- *"Crab dances in the background"*
- GC2 squeezes Pidd again. Pidd: *"NOOO! NO! LET ME GO! AA"*
- Pidd, in desperation, screams so hard it triggers her Draconic Cry (an ancient power she didn't know she had), distracting all three crabs
- Pidd kicks a crab attempting to flee. *"\*hurt her own little foot\**
- Pidd's final move: throws two acid vials at the crab holding Throm

**Why it works for comics:** The Pidd crab panic is a complete 8-panel comedic arc with internal escalation: grabbed → screams → breaks free → grabbed again → someone else punches the shell and hurts themselves → crab checks on another crab → screams louder → accidentally unlocks ancient power → hurts own foot. The *"AAAA! AAA! BIG CRABS!! HELP!"* is the opening panel. The *"hurt her own little foot"* is the character beat. The acid vials as final solution is the punchline.

Pidd previously appeared in `0689197d` (hot spring escape, eldritch duck). Her panic register is consistent.
