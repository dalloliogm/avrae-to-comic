## Highlighted Scene 1: Corryn + Orance Vampire Hunt

**Setup:** Aasimar paladin/sorcerer Corryn and half-orc barbarian Orance hunt vampire spawns in an unnamed location. They communicate about tactics, heal each other, and take damage across multiple rounds. After the spawn group is defeated, Corryn reveals why they came.

**Beats:**
- Corryn to Orance (tactical): *"That will seal the damage so they can't heal."* (about radiant damage)
- Orance: *"Thanks! But man these vampires are tough!"*
- Corryn: *"this is just their spawn!… let me know if you need healing, but we need to keep blasting so they don't heal!"*
- Orance gets drained twice; Corryn takes a bite and gets drained
- Master vampire appears mid-fight: *"I am the one you seek… and you just killed two of my brides and my valet!"*
- Victory; emotional coda:
- Corryn: *"good work! I love strong women! Glad you were here"*
- Corryn: *"well I'm glad you saw Renly and followed…. Needed you today!"* → *"I found out about this place… my parents were killed by vampires…."*

**Why it works for comics:** The backstory reveal after the fight is a clean emotional payload — it reframes the entire mission. *"I love strong women!"* is a warmth beat between partners. The master vampire's entrance mid-fight is a strong page-turn moment.

## Highlighted Scene 2: Vanessa Parker Guardian Angel

**Setup:** Blood Hunter Vanessa Parker goes through a solo extended encounter that evolves into a GM-narrated power fantasy. She keeps fighting through a sequence of increasingly supernatural challenges until she is described as *"literally becoming an angelic guardian."*

**Beats:**
- Fight escalates to godlike power
- GM narrates inner transformation: *"you feel Empowered, as you mow down all the monsters with ease you see the children behind you, they look so happy so calm, so safe, you keep chanting 'ANGEL!, ANGEL!, ANGEL!'"*
- Vanessa flies toward new screams: *"She would let no innocents fall today. In this state, she was an angel, she was to protect."*
- Saves a dying adult + children from monsters; heals them; calms their hearts
- Vision coda (mentor voice): *"great job vanessa!, although this might not be entirely real, i hope this will motivate you a little, you can get this strong and i know that you will, you can become a guardian angel!!"*

**Why it works for comics:** The angel sequence is a dream-within-a-fight. The tonal shift from blood hunter to angelic protector is visually strong. But it's heavily GM-narrated — the player character has limited direct voice. This works better as an adaptation challenge than a direct transcription.
