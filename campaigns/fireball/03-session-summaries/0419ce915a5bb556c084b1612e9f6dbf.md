- Clorox tanks all three Ettins' attacks through the fight
- One Ettin is killed; Clorox **absorbs its severed head into its plasmoid form** and continues

**Why it works for comics:** The horse dying in panel one recontextualizes the rest — Clorox is now alone, outnumbered, fighting from the ground.
