**Setup:** In a multi-party fight against four snake enemies (SnakeMomma 1-4), half-elf rogue/fighter Tenebril WolfHeart misses his katana attacks repeatedly across multiple turns.

**Beats:**
- Miss 1: *"Moves back to the group... tempted to throw it into the pit"*
- Miss 2: Moves to SnakeMomma 2; misses again
- Miss 3: *"*Looks at his katana concerned, takes a deep breath, walks forward and swings*"* — misses
- After 4th miss: *"*Sheathes it. BA disengage and walks away 15 ft with a small tear in his eye, seemingly unconcerned with the snake anymore*"*
- *"Yes sir. I don't know what's wrong. I think the damn thing is broken."*
- Final: *"It seems I may need it but for now she will be resting in her sheathe. I've lost my trust in her... He says sadly"*

**Why it works for comics:** Pure character comedy through accumulated humiliation. The weapon becomes a character.
