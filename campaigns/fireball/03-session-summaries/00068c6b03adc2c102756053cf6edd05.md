## Best Scene: Oni Child-Hostage Warehouse (turns ~35–65)

**Setup:** The party enters a warehouse to find dozens of children's bones stacked high, two living girls (Hanrietta and Zoey) tied to glaives jabbed into the pile. An elderly man holds a dagger to Hanrietta's throat. Then the twist: Hanrietta was feigning distress — her hands erupt in ice alongside the man's, and both cast Cone of Cold at the party simultaneously. All three figures (man, guard, "hostage") grow into giant Oni.

**Key beats:**
- Val'trex tries to teleport Zoey to safety via Vortex Warp; Zoey is dragged back by the guard
- Party realises the glaives in the bone pile are also weapons — the Oni grow with their weapons
- Nimue lands Hold Monster on one Oni, paralysing it; the party piles on crits
- Val fires a Wand of Magic Missiles at a fleeing gaseous-form Oni, screaming in Giant: `"WITCH, WITCH SAVE ME, WE HAD A DEAL!!"`
- Oni are revealed to be child-eating bogeymen; the DM notes every child bone is picked clean

**OOC level:** Moderate. Several interruptions for map confusion and timing clarifications, but IC narration is sustained and vivid throughout.

**Comic potential:** High. Clear three-act structure (rescue → twist → fight), strong villain reveal, bone-pile set design, multiple visual set pieces (ice hands, growing Oni, magic missiles).
