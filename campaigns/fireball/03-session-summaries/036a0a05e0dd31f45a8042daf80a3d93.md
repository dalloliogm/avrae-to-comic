## Highlighted Scene 1: The Graceful Exit

**Setup:** Three incubi attempt to charm the party. The party resists all attempts. The incubi, finding no purchase, execute a graceful retreat without apparent embarrassment.

**Beats:**
1. Incubus 1 approaches the nearest party member; charm attempt; resisted
2. Incubus 2 tries a different target; resisted again
3. Incubus 3 does not attempt the third; simply watches
4. Brief conference between the three — described as a murmur, not panicked
5. Incubus 1 (apparently the lead) turns back to the party: *"Well. You are a resilient group."*
6. He bows — an actual, correct formal bow
7. *"We're sure to take you up on that invitation some other time."*
8. No one invited them to anything. They leave.

**Why it works for comics:** The last line is the beat — *"We're sure to take you up on that invitation some other time"* delivered with complete composure after failing entirely is a confidence gag with a clean visual: three incubi walking away with dignity intact, party standing baffled behind them. Zero threat, maximum poise.

**Beats:**

2. Gundradyn — massive, golden — surveys the aftermath
3. The dragon bends down and picks Theodas up with obvious care — described as cradling him
4. Another party member asks if Theodas will be alright
5. Gundradyn: *"He will be fine."* Beat. *"I have done this before."*
