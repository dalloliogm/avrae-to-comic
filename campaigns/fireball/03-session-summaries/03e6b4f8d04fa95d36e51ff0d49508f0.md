**Beats:**
- Fae grappled and dragged to Zone 3 by tentacle (22 damage)
- Fae casts Dawn while still grappled; creature drops her to consume the spell
- Fae immediately casts Command: *"Grovel at my feet bitch"*
- Creature fails save — then promptly tentacle-crits her for 47 damage anyway

**Why it works for comics:** The comedy of a powerful wizard insisting on dignity while getting repeatedly slapped by tentacles has great panel rhythm. *"Grovel at my feet bitch"* followed immediately by a crit is a perfect visual gag.
