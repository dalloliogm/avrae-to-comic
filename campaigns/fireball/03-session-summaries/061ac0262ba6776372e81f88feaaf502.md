## Highlighted Scene 1: Oda vs Cultist — The Body Stays Up

**Setup:** Tabaxi barbarian Oda charges a hooded cultist blocking a church entrance. He smashes the cultist's face clean off with his two-handed warhammer.

**Beats:**
- The hood falls. The man's face is visibly destroyed. The body does not fall.
- Su'nar Kaazar-Erisi: *"Wh-what **are** you?"* — arrow flies wide as his arm loses control from shock
- Reya Mantlemorn: *"Wha- Why is he still up? I ain't ever seen anything like it!"*
- The cultist raises his arm, draws a dagger, calmly opens his own bowels on the altar
- Blood flows into the ritual grooves. The body finally falls.

**Why it works for comics:** Four panels. Oda's triumphant swing → the body stands upright → Su'nar's horror close-up → the cultist's calm self-sacrifice. The horror comes from the body not falling when it should. The ritual payoff (blood into grooves) is one panel. No explanation needed.

## Highlighted Scene 2: Zillah + Arkus — Hunger of Hadar Embrace

**Setup:** Gem Dragonborn sorcerer Zillah and Aasimar bard Arkus are sparring. Something starts going wrong with Zillah — her attacks escalate, she begins casting Hunger of Hadar on Arkus, a writhing void that blinds him.

**Beats:**
- Arkus calls out from inside the darkness: *"Zillah! Stop this!! Something isn't right!!!"*
- Zillah keeps casting; Arkus keeps healing himself and shouting
- Finally, Arkus bursts from the darkness and wraps his arms tightly around Zillah, preventing her from moving
- *"Please, stop... I can get you the help you need..."*
- *"It's alright, it's safe... let's go to the temple, talk to Aeon..."*
- Zillah fires one last psychic breath — Arkus doesn't let go

**Why it works for comics:** The visual (someone emerging from a void of darkness specifically to embrace the person creating it) is immediately striking. The wings reveal (*"Arkus doesn't care that someone saw his real wings"*) adds weight. The final image is two people standing, arms locked, Arkus holding on.

## Highlighted Scene 3: Wren Flambeaux — Meal Sanctuary

**Setup:** Fire Genasi artificer Wren Flambeaux fights a Water Weird in an arena. The Weird is immune to fire and most things; Wren has an all-purpose tool and limited spells.

**Beat:**
- Water Weird reaches Wren, attempts to attack —
- Wren has cast Sanctuary on herself
- DM narration: *"the weird cannot comprehend the concept of not interrupting a meal, and is repelled by the sheer power of the idea"*
- Wren produces a bowl of rice. The Weird stops.

One absurdist panel. The giant water serpent halted by a bowl of rice.
