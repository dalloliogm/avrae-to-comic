## Highlighted Scene 1: Kaska — Grieving Leonin Arena Fight

**Setup:** Leonin ranger/paladin Kaska enters the arena alone with her snake companion Alecto. She is not here for glory. She is here to take out her anger on something, *"without fear or judgment."*

**Beats:**
- The undead thing sprays necrotic ink; blinds Kaska
- *"The disease did not hold and her sight did not matter. In her Mind's Eye she could still see her opponent."*
- She wins
- *"A quick victory but a shallow one for Kaska. As she and Alecto came back together, the leonin could not hear their cheering. Soberly she collects her winnings, freeing the space for those who wanted to fight out of joy. Not to escape."*

**Why it works for comics:** A solo gladiator panel sequence — she enters arena, fights blind, wins, walks out silent. The contrast between crowd noise and her silence is the ending. No dialogue needed; all narration.

## Highlighted Scene 3: Pidd + Eldritch Duck

**Setup:** Kobold Pidd is cornered by Shadar-kai fighter Orga. She throws daggers, fails to escape, and hides in a hot spring. An apparently ordinary duck appears above them with eldritch tendrils extending from its feet and beak.

**Beats:**
- Pidd: *"h-helppp..."* — swimming desperately across the spring
- Orga sticks his head in the water looking for her
- Duck: hovers 35ft up, *QUACK!*
- Duck has **the very same tendrils Orga possesses** 
- Orga holds action watching it; duck also holds action watching him
- *"The duck uses its tendrils to grab onto Pidd, flying away with incredible speed"*
- *"And with that, they just left the scene."*

**Why it works for comics:** The standoff between a man and a duck, both holding readied actions, both with eldrich tendrils, is surreal. The duck wins by simply flying away.
