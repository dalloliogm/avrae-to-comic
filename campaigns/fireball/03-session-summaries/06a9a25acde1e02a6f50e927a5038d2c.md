## Highlighted Scene: Danpher + Orga — Weapon Swap Spar

**Setup:** Kobold rogue Danpher Sikar and Shadar-kai fighter Orga fight alongside an Air Elemental together (Orga's summon?), then once it falls, turn on each other. Danpher keeps swapping weapons every attack — bow, then drops it; dagger, then grapples with it; bow again; then drops the bow and grapples.

**Beats:**
- Air Elemental falls; Danpher: *"Killed your air thing. Now it's me and you."*
- Drops his bow, flicks out a dagger
- Attacks with dagger → drops it → tries to grapple — surprises Orga
- *"a surprisingly strong grab!"*
- Orga tries to hit Danpher; Danpher dodges both
- *"he somehow dodges both!"*
- Danpher then drops the remaining dagger and flicks out his bow again from the grapple
- Danpher, after taking several big hits: *"Hey im close enough to unconscious"* [coughs]

**Why it works for comics:** The visual comedy of watching someone swap weapons every single turn — bow → dagger → grapple → bow again — while being in the middle of a fight. The cough-line closer is perfect. Orga standing there going *"a surprisingly strong grab"* while a kobold switches to their third weapon is a running gag in a single fight.
