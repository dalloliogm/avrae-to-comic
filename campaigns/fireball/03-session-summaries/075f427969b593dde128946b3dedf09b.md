## Highlighted Scene 1: Atramir vs Tiamat

**Setup:** Protector Aasimar artificer Atramir Decimus Toran faces Tiamat (the actual dragon god) alone. He has a halberd, a Banishing Smite, and boundless optimism.

**Beats:**
- Atramir, approaching: *"Bahamut is here? Nice to meet you by the way Bahamut. I'm a big fan."*
- Tiamat (not Bahamut): *"I commend your bravery. I will make you an aspect of mine when this ends."*
- Atramir: *"I'm a big fan"*
- Tiamat's legendary action: Acid Breath, 71 damage
- Atramir (still standing): *"It does sting quite a bit"*
- Tiamat: *"I'll make this painful."* — Claw, then crit, then Divine Word
- Atramir goes down
- Atramir immediately casts Revivify on Talia who also went down: *"Atramir runs over to Talia and resurrects her"*
- Nix: *"He helps her up, 'You get the chance to meet death?' / 'It's normally not a good idea'"*
- Atramir then Cure Wounds + Arcane Jolt on Talia: *"You're really that selfish to not want to stop this? A lot of people died under her attack..."*

**Why it works for comics:** The tone is perfect throughout: enthusiastic fan meets the actual evil dragon god, compliments Bahamut who is not present, gets pulverized, immediately spends his first action after revival healing someone else. The *"Nice to meet you"* line is the signature moment — Atramir experiencing Tiamat as a celebrity encounter rather than an existential threat.
