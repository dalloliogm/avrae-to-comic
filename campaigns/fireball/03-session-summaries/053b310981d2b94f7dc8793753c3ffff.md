## Highlighted Scene 1: Krin Well + VonWick Son — Underwater Spar

**Setup:** Krin Well (monk/barbarian) invites VonWick Son (half-orc fighter) to train in the ocean. Both wade in and fight on the seafloor. Almost no dialogue — just Avrae rolls and physical narration.

**Beats:**
- Krin stands on the sandy floor, staff ready; VonWick descends
- Both fighting while managing breath
- Sand mushrooms up around missed attacks; bubbles trail from impacts
- Krin's staff cracks what sounds like VonWick's rib; VonWick loses his breath from the pain; starts swallowing water
- Krin gives VonWick a thumbs up mid-fight before continuing
- VonWick blacks out; wakes on shore watching blue sky and seagulls
- Krin: *"Ah... you finally awake."*

**Why it works for comics:** The seafloor panel — sand clouds, bubble streams, two silhouettes — does all the work. The thumbs-up mid-fight is the perfect comic beat. Waking on shore as a closer needs only two panels. Wordless except for the ending line.

- Elemental grapples Matthew repeatedly; Oriben pulls him out each time
- Oriben's healing word: *"And the word of healing today is.... LIVE"*
- Matthew uses Thunder Step to teleport out of a grapple, landing beside Oriben
- Oriben: *"I'll save my other two slots for healing you!"*

**Why it works for comics:** The Telepathy link (Matthew and Oriben communicate without speaking mid-fight) is a clean visual device — speech bubbles only one character can hear.
