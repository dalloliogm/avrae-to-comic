## Highlighted Scene 1: Ludwick — Portal Balor Reaction

**Setup:** Harengon wizard/ranger Ludwick Vern Velowim and Dhampir paladin/warlock Dulacar step through a portal. On the other side: three scaled creatures surrounding a Balor (a massive demon with bat wings, flaming whip, and a sword). It points the sword directly at them.

**Beats:**
- Ludwick: *"A-ah... w-well, then... that's uh... that's a bit of a thing, huh?"* — flashes Dulacar an awkward grin
- Immediately activates Nature's Veil (goes invisible), teleports sideways, and starts shooting from range
- Ludwick: *"I mean, uh... oh nooo~"* — pretend-dismayed while immediately effective
- Balor has Truesight; can see him anyway
- Ludwick: *"C-copy my moves eh? Then how about this!"* — bounds away as a bullet, fires several shots mid-air, lands further back

**Why it works for comics:** The nervous reaction + instant tactical competence is the entire character in two panels. He processes the situation (*"that's a bit of a thing"*), tells Dulacar he's fine, and is already invisible before the Balor can move. *"Oh nooo~"* while actively winning is perfect comic register.
