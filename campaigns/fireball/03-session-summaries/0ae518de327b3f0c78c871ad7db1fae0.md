## Highlighted Scene 1: Nue — Dragon Taunt

**Setup:** A dragon breathes fire. Nue, facing it, responds.

**Beat:**
- Nue: *"I can spit stuff at people too! Stupid fat lizard!"*
- Dragon incinerates her.

Two panels. The defiance is the whole point — she knows she can't win this exchange and does it anyway. *"Stupid fat lizard"* is perfect: not a calculated insult, just direct. The next panel is fire.

**Why it works for comics:** Characters who get beaten immediately after talking trash are a reliable beat, but the *"I can spit stuff at people too"* framing makes Nue feel like she genuinely thought this was going somewhere. She didn't. The dragon's fire breath is the punchline.
