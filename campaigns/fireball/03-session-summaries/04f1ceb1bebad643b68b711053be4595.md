## Highlighted Scene 1: Red Wolf vs Eloquent — Emotional Confrontation

**Setup:** Red Wolf (monk) attacks Eloquent (warlock) in grief and rage over someone named "Flame." Other party members try to intervene: Kitsuna Foxheart joins the fight, Brettyn mediates, Nathaniel holds Kit back.

**Beats:**
- Red Wolf attacks Eloquent: *"ITS BECAUSE YOU WONT LET YOURSELF HEAL!!"*
- Eloquent: *"I am not sorry for what I did. Just for what it continues to do"*
- Kitsuna: *"I will not fight back."* [attacks anyway]
- Nathaniel wraps his wings around Kit: *"My mother was killed infront of me when I was a hatchling... My family, my lover... my unborn child Kit. Everything was lost... Let me stand for you."*
- Brettyn: *"No one needs to die tonight"*
- Resolution: everyone restrained, healed, and tied up together

**Why it works for comics:** The five-person tangle (two fighters, one trying to intervene, one trying to stop the intervener, one mediating) is visually chaotic but emotionally coherent. Nathaniel's monologue is the pivot. The scene ends on a strange warmth — everyone tied up, everyone alive.

## Highlighted Scene 2: Val'trex + Amity — Rat Fight Aftermath

**Beats:**

- Amity, discovering her ankles are shredded: *"Shit... this is what I get for trying to show off to a fucking guy"* [does not realize Val can hear her]
- Infection discovered; Val: *"Fuck. Okay, I have a friend who might be able to cure it. If not, I'm taking you back to the Druids and I'm paying for it. No objections."*
- Val, when thanked: *"I... just needed to move you into a timeline that didn't end with your grisly demise."*

**Why it works for comics:** The *"showing off to a fucking guy"* line followed by him having clearly heard it is one good beat. Val's timeline-rewind explanation is a perfect callback to show why the rats never reached her. The dynamic (competent artificer who won't admit she was flustered; competent wizard who won't admit he panicked) is clean.

## Highlighted Scene 3: Wren + Aisling — Chaos Sneezes + Carry-Out

**Setup:** Eladrin sorcerer Wren has a Feywild Shard that triggers Wild Magic Surges when she uses Metamagic. During the fight, she sneezes so hard her hair falls out repeatedly (wild magic effect). Aisling keeps healing her.

**Beats:**
- Wren sneezes 5+ times during combat; hair falls out each time
- Wren: *"Fuck!! Ais I'm so sorry."* [after a surge hurts Aisling]
- Aisling: *"You're okay. We both are."*
- Post-fight, Wren is overwhelmed; holding back tears; reaches for her hair, finds none
- Aisling crouches in front of her, wiggles hands: *"I'm fine. Now get up here before you make me sad."*
- Wren: *"That's not fair. You know I can't—"* [pouts, climbs on Aisling's back like a koala]
- Aisling carries her out: *"Watch from our room? The window by the bed has a really nice view."*
- Wren: *"Really? I hadn't noticed. Okay, yeah."*

**Why it works for comics:** The carry-out is a 3-panel closer: Wren pouts, Wren koalas onto Aisling, they walk out. The hairless Wren hugging the taller Aisling is a strong visual. The fight context (chaos surges, sneezes) gives the tenderness its weight.
