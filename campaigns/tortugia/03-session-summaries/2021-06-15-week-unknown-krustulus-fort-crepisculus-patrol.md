## Summary

Krustulus was stationed at Fort Crepisculus and had been doing heavy labor because his strength was noticed. His commanding officer assigned him to his first patrol lead: take a small group north for half a day along the trail to New Xanadu and meet wagons traveling from there. Recent gnoll and Sahuagin activity had made extra escort necessary.

The final patrol composition was 4 guards, 1 scout, and 1 soldier. The named NPCs introduced include Vashai / Sarge, Tarak, Bathin, Kazim, and Maccus, with an additional air genasi archer nearby.

## Durable Facts

- Krustulus was level 6 in this sequence.
- Fort Crepisculus maintained patrols toward New Xanadu.
- Gnolls and Sahuagins were active threats near the Fort Crepisculus / New Xanadu route.
- Krustulus had enough trust or responsibility to lead a patrol.
