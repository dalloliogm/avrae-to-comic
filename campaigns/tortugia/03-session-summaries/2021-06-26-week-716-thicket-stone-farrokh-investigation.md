# Week 716: Thicket And The Stone Discuss Farrokh

- Source log: `../02-chat-logs/2021-06-26-week-716-thicket-stone-farrokh-investigation.md`
- Source date: 2021-06-26 to 2021-06-30
- Location: Silent Fortress throne room, later Cayona
- Characters: Thicket, The Stone

## Summary

The Stone invited Thicket into the Silent Fortress throne room for a private meeting. After putting the crown on and experiencing the strange perception that comes with it, The Stone asked why Thicket had spoken in front of Artus about the thing he wanted to research. Thicket admitted this was unwise. The Stone reminded him that Thicket had warned months earlier that confronting people present when Farrokh died or disappeared could be dangerous, and Artus was one of those people.

The Stone said that, according to various people, several individuals were present and had worked hard to hide their tracks or avoid consequences. Thicket identified Salish, Rezkin, Artus, and Felix as people who were there or should be questioned. Thicket said Farrokh must not be alive anymore and that stories of escape to the Feywild were lies.

They discussed contacting Razafaelnut in Espera. Thicket knew Raza from when Raza came to Tortugia and met Farrokh, but had not seen him since Farrokh's disappearance. The Stone had heard of two failed attempts to meet Raza, both involving deception. Vergwen may have a contact who sends messages but cannot be contacted back.

Thicket listed Farrokh's important possessions: the Cli Lyre, Thulwar's spellbook, night sunglasses/goggles, and a ring of spell storing. The Stone asked Thicket to try Locate Object in the throne room. Thicket cast it for the Cli Lyre, but nothing was detected. Thicket could not confirm that Salish's Goggles of Night were Farrokh's.

The two then discussed traveling to Espera, whether to bring Vergwen or Willibald, and the danger of approaching Raza without warning. The Stone also asked Thicket for help with leadership logistics, especially distributing seasonal payments to crew leaders or Round Table members through Thicket's shop. Thicket agreed and asked that compensation for about 300 gp lost on a prior expedition be handled as guild influence if possible.

## Durable Facts

- Thicket believes Farrokh was trapped by the old king and later killed.
- The Stone also believes Farrokh was likely killed.
- Named potential witnesses/suspects: Salish, Rezkin, Artus, Felix.
- Farrokh's key items: Cli Lyre, Thulwar's spellbook, night sunglasses/goggles, ring of spell storing.
- Locate Object in the Silent Fortress throne room did not find the Cli Lyre.
- Raza / Razafaelnut is in Espera but difficult to contact.
- The Stone trusts Thicket enough to discuss sensitive Farrokh matters and practical governance support.

## Avrae / Mechanics

- Thicket used a mundane activity for meeting with the king on the first day of Week 716.
- The Stone used mundane activities for the same meeting.
- Thicket cast Locate Object. Spell limitations discussed: 1,000 feet, blocked by any thickness of lead.
- Thicket estimated he could cast Locate Object about five times per day.
