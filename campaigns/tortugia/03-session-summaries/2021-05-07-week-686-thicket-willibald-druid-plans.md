# Week 686: Thicket And Willibald Discuss Druid Plans

- Source log: `../02-chat-logs/2021-05-07-week-686-thicket-willibald-druid-plans.md`
- Source date: 2021-05-07 to 2021-05-10
- Location: Port-Leroy
- Characters: Thicket, Willibald Bramblethorn

## Summary

Thicket met Willibald Bramblethorn, the self-described Archdruid of Duskburg and leader of the local druid circle. They spoke in Druidic. Thicket explained that he was not new to Tortugia: he had lived in Port-Leroy while helping set up Concetta's shop, then retreated to Espera after Farrokh disappeared because Concetta's health was poor.

Willibald said some Espera druids had asked him to investigate which boat Farrokh left on. If a boat record showed Farrokh was exiled, that would support survival; otherwise the suspicion that he was killed would remain. Thicket pushed back on the exile theory, saying Farrokh went to meet the old king, never came back, and sent a message calling for help because the king had trapped him.

Willibald described a larger plan: resolve the Farrokh question, ally the new King Stone with Razafaelnut, possibly travel to Belfegor's lands in the Chalician peninsula to enrich the land and seek an alliance, and eventually gather enough support to defeat Grenel. He also wanted community gardens in Duskburg to help with the refugee crisis.

Thicket explained that The Stone had asked him to help Willibald with Belfegor. Thicket described Belfegor as like a storm, queen of chaos and sudden change, and wondered if Belfegor might be a druid because she had sent a druid messenger to King Salish. Willibald and Thicket considered traveling together first, then bringing more people if Belfegor proved receptive.

## Durable Facts

- Willibald Bramblethorn leads Duskburg's druid circle.
- Some Espera druids suspected Farrokh was killed rather than exiled.
- Willibald was investigating boating records to test the exile story.
- Thicket says Farrokh sent a message that the old king trapped him.
- The Stone asked Thicket to help Willibald with Belfegor.
- Willibald had plans for Duskburg community gardens amid a refugee crisis.
