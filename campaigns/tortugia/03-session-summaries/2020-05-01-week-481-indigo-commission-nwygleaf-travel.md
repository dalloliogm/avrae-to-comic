## Summary

Bordan Doom, Zefyr, Krustulus, and Aurath signed onto a mission at the Nwygleaf Wood encampment of the Indigo Prince's commission. Krustulus opened with cheerful music and conversation, performing badly at first because the piano needed tuning, then very well afterward.

The group discussed Prince Taristar, the Spider Queen, and the possibility of Taristar as a pacifist king. Bordan described himself as an old army general who once thirsted for battle but now wants to see what peace can do for people in Tortugia. Zefyr distributed healing resources before the journey, including goodberries and a healing potion for Bordan and Aurath, and even berries for the flying ettercap on Aurath's shoulder.

The group embarked from the docks on a longboat operated by Prince Taristar's men. The journey to Nwygleaf Woods took three days in freezing temperatures, snow, choppy seas, and persistent northeast wind. A long rest/camp instruction block followed for the first night.

## Durable Facts
