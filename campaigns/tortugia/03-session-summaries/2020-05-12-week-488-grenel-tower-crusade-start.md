# Week 488: Grenel Tower Crusade Start

- Location: Prince Lucien's estate in Cayona, then Serfiolan Grave swamp encampment
- Characters: Lucien, Nissa Ningel, Langston, Vail Kaerdun, Za'al, Malec, Mallet, Krustulus
- Privacy: Source explicitly says this is a secret mission channel and player-knowledge only.

## Summary

Prince Lucien gathered a large group of Rovers for a crusade against the undead threat of the Necromancer Grenel at Grenel's Tower, located at the western tip of the Serfiolan swamps. Krustulus arrived cheerfully with a harpsichord, played music while the group waited, and declared the mission glorious in Gruumsh's name.

Lucien warned that the mission targeted undead, ghouls, and Grenel herself, whom he described as ancient and connected to the previous King of Tortugia. He said Grenel had threatened to skin his men if he returned, and gave participants a last chance to turn back.

Lucien then used a violet-sliver teleportation ritual involving a stone brought back by previous shifts at Serfiolan Graves. After about an hour, Lucien, the adventurers, and Ishim his bodyguard teleported from the Cayona estate to the swamp encampment. The camp's rover roughnecks gathered for orders. Lucien told them the land must be cleansed and declared the goal of killing Grenel.

## Durable Facts

- Grenel's Tower is at the western tip of the Serfiolan swamps.
- Prince Lucien considered Grenel ancient and tied to a previous King of Tortugia.
- Grenel had threatened Lucien's men if he returned.
- Lucien could use his violet sliver for a teleportation ritual keyed by a stone from the destination.
- Serfiolan Grave camp was about 60 miles southeast of Grenel Tower, roughly a two-day journey along a previously traveled path.
- Ishim served as Lucien's bodyguard.
