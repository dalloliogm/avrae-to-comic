# Tortugia Comic 00: Page 6 Script

## Page Goal

Return to the new Rover and launch the campaign premise: this is a living kingdom with freedom, danger, factions, and unfinished history.

## Layout

- Six panels.
- Panel 6 is the closing image.

---

## Panel 1

Present-day Port-Leroy docks. Rain, lanterns, trade crates, ropes, cutters, longboats, mixed fantasy peoples, off-worlders, Rovers, workers, and guards. The new Rover stands at the edge of the dock, taking it in.

**Caption:** `Port-Leroy receives those the mists deliver.`

**Art Notes:** Make the dock busy without becoming unreadable. Tortugia is a melting pot.

**Prompt:** Crowded Port-Leroy fantasy harbor in rain and lantern light, cutters and longboats, mixed fantasy peoples and sailors, new traveler standing stunned on dock, dark maritime fantasy comic panel.

---

## Panel 2

The Harbor Guide walks beside the new Rover. Behind them, a cutter crew secures ropes carefully. A low longboat with round shields along its side passes in the background.

**New Rover:** `Can I go home?`

**Harbor Guide:** `Aye. If you learn the mists.`

**Art Notes:** The answer is honest but incomplete.

**Prompt:** Veteran sailor guide walking with new fantasy traveler on wet harbor dock, cutter crew securing ropes, shield-lined longboat passing behind, lantern-lit rain, comic panel.

---

## Panel 3

Close on the Harbor Guide's face or hands as they gesture toward the monolith's distant beam beyond the harbor.

**Harbor Guide:** `If you survive the sea.`

**Harbor Guide:** `If Tortugia lets you sleep long enough to forget it.`

**Art Notes:** The last line should feel like practical warning, not melodrama.

**Prompt:** Weathered fantasy sailor gesturing toward distant white monolith beam through harbor mist, rain on face, practical warning mood, maritime comic panel.

---

## Panel 4

Map-like threat montage hovering over water or in the Rover's imagination: Grenel's ghouls beyond an eastern wall, Shelobb's nightmare eyes over Nalfgard and Espera, a raven-dark silhouette over the shadowmists.

**Caption:** `Beyond the docks, old powers stir.`

**Caption:** `Grenel. Shelobb. The Raven Queen's shadow.`

**Art Notes:** Keep the Raven Queen off-panel and symbolic unless later visual canon is chosen.

**Prompt:** Stylized fantasy map threat montage, undead ghouls beyond eastern wall, nightmare eyes over frontier lands, raven-shaped shadow over rainbow mists, dark chronicle comic panel.

---

## Panel 5

The Council of Seven in silhouette beneath an empty crown. The panel can be seen as an official chamber, a rumor, or a symbolic overlay above the city.

**Caption:** `A council rules where a king is missing.`

**Art Notes:** Do not identify council members without source support.

**Prompt:** Seven council silhouettes seated beneath an empty crown symbol, dark fantasy political chamber, misty overlay, solemn comic panel.

---

## Panel 6

Final panel. The new Rover stands on a high dock or harbor stair, looking from Port-Leroy toward the distant Silent Fortress. Behind them through mist, the monolith's white beam glows. The rainbow color is faint but present at the edge of the fog.

**Caption:** `Welcome to Tortugia.`

**Caption:** `The next name in its history has not been written yet.`

**No dialogue.**

**Art Notes:** This should feel like an invitation and a warning.

**Prompt:** New fantasy Rover standing on wet harbor stair at Port-Leroy, looking toward distant brooding Silent Fortress above volcanic island, white monolith beam glowing behind through rainbow mist, cinematic final comic panel.

## Lettering Notes

- Keep the final two captions separated, with the last one low on the page.
- Page 6 should end with silence after the captions.

## Continuity Notes

- Rovers can cross the mists, but prolonged absence from Tortugia brings restless dreams and dangerous consequences.
- Current threats are shown at a high level only; no private-note details are used.
