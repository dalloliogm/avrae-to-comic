# Tortugia Comic 00: Page 3 Script

## Page Goal

Deliver the first full view of Tortugia: beautiful, dangerous, volcanic, maritime, and civilized despite the nightmare crossing.

## Layout

- Four panels.
- Panel 1 is a half-page establishing splash.

---

## Panel 1

Half-page wide splash. Tortugia emerges beyond the monolith. Red volcanic glow marks the chain of Los Diablos. A large city sprawls from sea level to the mid-slope. Port-Leroy's harbor lights cluster near the water. Above the city, the Silent Fortress broods in black silhouette where the rainbow arc seems to end.

**Caption:** `Tortugia: a kingdom between planes.`

**Caption:** `Shadowfell-born. Fire-scarred. Sea-bound.`

**Art Notes:** This is the cover-image candidate. It needs clean geography: monolith, sea, harbor, city, volcanoes, fortress.

**Prompt:** Wide establishing comic splash of Tortugia, volcanic island demi-plane beyond fog, red glow of active volcano chain, Port-Leroy harbor lights, city sprawling from sea level up a slope, brooding black fortress above, rainbow arc from monolith to fortress, dark maritime fantasy.

---

## Panel 2

A high-sided cutter emerges from the shadowmist, lanterns swinging, crew watching the pulsing monolith. The cutter's sides are tall enough to feel like protection against things in the water.

**Caption:** `Cutters cross the shadowmists and return.`

**Art Notes:** Show the ship as practical and enchanted rather than ornate.

**Prompt:** High-sided enchanted cutter ship emerging from rainbow shadowmist, lanterns, skilled crew watching a pulsing black monolith, practical pirate fantasy vessel, comic panel.

---

## Panel 3

Below the waterline or through a wave cutaway: sea-devil silhouettes circle the rocks, eyeing loose ropes and the hull. Above, a sailor hauls a rope in sharply.

**Caption:** `Provided no one grows careless near the sea.`

**Art Notes:** This ties to the cutter note that sea devils cannot board if no loose ropes are left hanging.

**Prompt:** Split waterline comic panel, sea-devil silhouettes circling under a cutter near rocks, sailor hauling loose rope above, tense maritime fantasy, lantern light and fog.

---

## Panel 4

The Rover is guided onto the cutter or into harbor hands. A veteran crew member grips their forearm. The Rover is drenched, stunned, and looking past the rescuer toward the city.

**Harbor Guide:** `First time?`

**New Rover:** `I think I was dreaming.`

**Harbor Guide:** `Aye. That's how it starts.`

**Art Notes:** Keep this human-scale after the splash. The audience needs a point of emotional contact.

**Prompt:** Veteran mist-wet sailor helping stunned fantasy traveler aboard a cutter near Port-Leroy, fog, lanterns, distant volcanic island and fortress, cinematic fantasy comic panel.

## Lettering Notes

- Panel 1 captions should be placed high or low, not across the fortress.
- Spoken balloons in Panel 4 should be small and grounded.

## Continuity Notes

- Port-Leroy is a major harbor and arrival point.
- Cutters are the core ship type for crossing shadowmists and carrying off-worlders.
