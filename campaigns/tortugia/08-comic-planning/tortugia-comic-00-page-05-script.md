# Tortugia Comic 00: Page 5 Script

## Page Goal

Bridge ancient myth to current instability: the Rainbow Omen, Salish, Xarkus, The Stone, Artus, and the closed Silent Fortress.

## Layout

- Six panels.
- Fast chronicle montage with one quiet final panel.

---

## Panel 1

The Silent Fortress above Cayona. After centuries of brooding closure, a rainbow omen blazes at its top, reflected in clouds and wet black stone.

**Caption:** `After 900 years, a rainbow burned above the Silent Fortress.`

**Art Notes:** The fortress should feel like an institution waking.

**Prompt:** Brooding black basalt fortress above crags, rainbow omen blazing at the top, rain clouds and morning light, epic fantasy comic panel.

---

## Panel 2

Seven colored Prince and Princess silhouettes converge from different directions: ships, mounts, roads, and citadel shadows. Do not render them as final character designs.

**Caption:** `The shard-holders came to claim Ogeron's throne.`

**Art Notes:** Keep this symbolic and historical. The source marks many prince mechanics as historical.

**Prompt:** Seven colored fantasy ruler silhouettes converging toward a black fortress from ships, roads, and sky, symbolic chronicle comic panel, rainbow motif.

---

## Panel 3

Mythic duel silhouette: Salish on the Ki-rin Tian against Xarkus on the red dragon Ashardon. Dragon fire and radiant motion clash near the fortress.

**Caption:** `Salish and Tian defeated Xarkus and Ashardon.`

**Art Notes:** Distant legend image, not literal blow-by-blow.

**Prompt:** Mythic fantasy duel silhouette, blue prince riding radiant Ki-rin against red dragon rider, dragon fire near black fortress, epic chronicle comic panel.

---

## Panel 4

The throne room of the Silent Fortress opens. Black basalt doors, magically lit candelabras held by unseen servants in the long hall, then morning sun entering a vast throne room. Ogeron's shimmering Kalashtar apparition speaks before assembled figures.

**Caption:** `The silent citadel spoke.`

**Ogeron:** `The seven must stand stronger together than I ever could alone.`

**Art Notes:** The Ogeron line is adapted from the source's speech. Keep it short for lettering.

**Prompt:** Black basalt fortress throne room opening to morning sun, magical candelabras, shimmering Kalashtar apparition before assembled fantasy figures, solemn epic comic panel.

---

## Panel 5

Three quick inset images in one horizontal strip: The Stone pulls the Staff of Power from The Henge; The Stone passes the crown to General Artus; Artus's silhouette disappears into mist near Espera.

**Caption:** `Kings rose. Kings yielded. Then one vanished.`

**Art Notes:** This panel compresses history. Make each inset iconic.

**Prompt:** Three-part fantasy chronicle montage, stone figure pulling a staff from standing stones, public crowning of armored general, crowned king silhouette disappearing into mist near western lands, comic strip panel.

---

## Panel 6

The Silent Fortress closed again, black against a gray sky. No people. A small empty crown shape or dark sliver-shaped absence is suggested in the foreground.

**Caption:** `The King's Sliver vanished with Artus.`

**Caption:** `The Council of Seven now governs an uncertain future.`

**Art Notes:** End quiet and ominous.

**Prompt:** Silent black basalt fortress closed under gray rain sky, empty crown silhouette in foreground, mist, solemn dark fantasy comic panel.

## Lettering Notes

- Page 5 is the densest lore page. Keep captions short and declarative.
- Ogeron's line may use a distinct balloon style: shimmering caption-like speech, not normal dialogue.

## Continuity Notes

- Artus was last seen near Edilos in Espera; avoid showing an unsupported specific cause of disappearance.
- The Council of Seven forms after Artus's disappearance and the missing King's Sliver.
