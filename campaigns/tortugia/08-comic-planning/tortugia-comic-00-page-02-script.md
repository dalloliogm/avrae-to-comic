# Tortugia Comic 00: Page 2 Script

## Page Goal

Turn the dream into a voyage. The reader should feel the rules changing: bedroom to boat, memory to mist, private fear to maritime danger.

## Layout

- Five panels.
- Panel 4 should dominate the page.

---

## Panel 1

The bedroom dissolves into black water. The bed's blanket becomes the edge of a small boat. The Rover sits inside it, soaked in mist, confused but silent. No visible oarsman is needed.

**Caption:** `The call does not ask permission.`

**Art Notes:** The transition should be surreal but readable.

**Prompt:** Bedroom dissolving into a small boat on black water, confused fantasy traveler seated alone, mist replacing floorboards, dreamlike maritime dark fantasy comic panel.

---

## Panel 2

The small boat glides through dense shadowmist. A pale rainbow appears ahead like a path half-seen through fog.

**Caption:** `It offers a way.`

**Art Notes:** The rainbow should be pale and ghostly, not cheerful.

**Prompt:** Small boat sailing through dense ocean fog, pale ghostly rainbow path ahead, black water, shadowmist, dark fantasy comic panel.

---

## Panel 3

The mist around the boat suggests impossible shapes: a tentacle curling too far away, teeth beneath the surface, an eye made of darkness. Nothing is confirmed; everything could be fog.

**Caption:** `And it hides what waits beside the way.`

**Art Notes:** Keep the silhouettes ambiguous. This should imply the danger beyond the monoliths and the sea-devil threat without overdefining the creature.

**Prompt:** Small fantasy boat in shadowmist with ambiguous tentacle and teeth silhouettes hidden in fog and black water, tense maritime horror comic panel.

---

## Panel 4

Large vertical reveal. The black monolith rises from rocks battered by violent waves, colossal beside the tiny boat. Its round opening emits a bright white beam upward. Rainbow mist coils around the rocks. The boat stays at a safe distance from the shoals.

**Caption:** `Seven such stones ring the kingdom.`

**Caption:** `Their lights guide sailors where stars cannot.`

**Art Notes:** Scale matters. The Rover and boat should be tiny.

**Prompt:** Colossal black triangular monolith rising from wave-battered rocks, tiny boat nearby, round opening at top emitting white beam, rainbow shadowmist, dangerous shoals, epic fantasy comic splash panel.

---

## Panel 5

The monolith's light pulses. In the distance, through thinning fog, a rainbow arc reaches toward a fortress high above an island. The Rover stares at it.

**Caption:** `When the light pulses, a Rover is waiting.`

**No dialogue.**

**Art Notes:** This is the bridge to Page 3's first full view of Tortugia.

**Prompt:** Pulsing monolith light in ocean fog, distant rainbow arc leading toward brooding fortress above an island, small boat foreground, dark maritime fantasy comic panel.

## Lettering Notes

- Captions should sit away from the monolith beam so the beam remains visually dominant.
- Let Panel 4 breathe. Avoid dialogue over the reveal.

## Continuity Notes

- Seven monoliths are around Tortugia, roughly equidistant, and used by sailors for guidance.
- The pulsing light signals a Rover waiting to be picked up.
