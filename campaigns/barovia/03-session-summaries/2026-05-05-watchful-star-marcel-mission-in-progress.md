# The Watchful Star / Marcel Mission - In Progress

Date: 2026-05-05 onward
Source logs: ../02-chat-logs/2026-05-05-watchful-star-marcel-mission-in-progress.md
Status: In progress

## One-Paragraph Recap

Pyxis gathered companions on a hill near Vallaki to investigate a strange star-like light that did not match normal celestial movement. Marcel arrived in full celestial mime-scholar style and turned the scene into a proper cosmic performance with Magic Mouth harmonics and lute music. Muriel Vinshaw of the Keepers of the Feather revealed that the light is not a star but an observer from beyond Barovia, and Ezmerelda d'Avenir guided the group toward Mount Baratok. At the summit, a griffon marked with Pyxis's analemma appeared as Pyxis's mount, and the party ascended through the mists to a plateau under a true sky full of stars. The Celestial Observer explained that Pyxis's stargazing pierced Barovia's veil, that one ambassador now serves the prison and another, Elias, is chained within it. Two gloomweavers then forced the corrupted angel Elias onto the plateau, beginning a combat in which the group is trying to survive the Dark Powers' servants and possibly free or spare Elias.

## What Happened

- Pyxis led an informal astronomy club gathering in the Wilds near the Vistani camp outside Vallaki.
- Widget completed a custom telescope for Pyxis, including autofocus, a finderscope, and a cup holder.
- Marcel arrived in star-decorated robes, using silent theatrical gestures, illusionary fireworks, Magic Mouth stones, and a Doss Lute to create the right atmosphere.
- A strange gap opened in Barovia's cloud cover, revealing a single light with an uncanny sense of depth and intention.
- Muriel Vinshaw appeared from a raven shape and introduced herself as part of the Keepers of the Feather.
- Muriel said the light is not a star, is watching, and is not from Barovia.
- Muriel connected the phenomenon to stories of observers, sentinels, and celestial intelligences watching boundaries between worlds.
- Muriel said another similar being appeared years ago but vanished before it could be investigated.
- The group agreed to pursue the mystery and followed Muriel to Ezmerelda d'Avenir at the nearby Vistani camp.
- Ezmerelda agreed to guide them toward a thinning point in the mists near Mount Baratok, but warned she will not cross into the mist herself.
- At dawn, the group set out through Vallaki, past Lake Zarovich, and toward Mount Baratok.
- Ezmerelda explained that the party must cross the final two hundred feet from the ridge by their own means, such as flight, levitation, or magic.
- Ezmerelda warned that the mists physically wear travelers down and can use guilt, regret, and private self-accusations against them.
- At the summit shelf, a griffon appeared with an analemma marking matching the celestial loop above Pyxis's heart.
- The griffon lowered itself in deference to Pyxis and offered itself as his flying mount.
- The DM clarified that the griffon is from Pyxis's Find Greater Steed tattoo, flavored through the scene.
- The party had to fly, levitate, or otherwise cross two hundred feet upward through the mists and make a DC 20 Constitution save.
- At the top, the group reached a standable plateau within the mists. Above it is a sky full of stars, unlike anything seen in Barovia, and the tracked "star" appears to be a presence rather than a star.
- Pyxis gave each companion a Feather Token before the ascent and encouraged the others while riding the analemma-marked griffon.
- The Celestial Observer spoke telepathically, telling the group that Pyxis's gaze pierced Barovia's veil and revealed a hidden prison to the observers.
- The observer said one ambassador abandoned her purpose and now willingly serves the prison, while a second was captured and still suffers within the veil.
- The captured being, Elias, was forced onto the plateau by two spear-bearing gloomweavers; he appears as a chained, mutilated angel with ruined wings and a broken iron halo.
- Inge slowed the two gloomweavers and Elias with chronomancy.
- Widget teleported one gloomweaver beside himself using paired darts, then Cringer bit it.
- Marcel emerged from the clouds riding a large animated tree and tried to support Widget's diplomacy with charm-like music, but the enemies appeared immune and rejected the performance.
- Elias released a radiant Fallen Glory shockwave, knocking one gloomweaver prone and also knocking Marcel and his animated tree prone.
- Two whispering creatures joined the fight, using regret-voices that echoed Inge's mist vision.
- Pyxis tried to Polymorph Elias to break or loosen the chains, but the spell failed.

## Character Decisions

- Marcel supported the investigation through performance and atmosphere rather than direct speech, framing the celestial mystery as a dramatic event.
- Marcel used music and creation magic to ascend through the mists, framing himself as the unseen dramatic music that had accompanied the mission while he was out of focus.
- Marcel failed the mist crossing Constitution save, gained one level of exhaustion, and arrived on one knee with a weak but committed thumbs up.
- Marcel later revealed he had animated a large tree as a flying mount/platform through College of Creation magic.
- Marcel attempted to bolster Widget's negotiation with charming music, but the guardians and angel appeared immune; he was visibly offended by the artistic rejection.
- Marcel and the animated tree were knocked prone by Elias's Fallen Glory shockwave.
- Pyxis chose to pursue answers about the phenomenon and its possible connection to his arrival in Barovia.
- Pyxis approached the analemma-marked griffon, recognized the shared marking, and accepted its deference as transportation upward.
- Pyxis learned that his gaze pierced Barovia's veil but was told he is not to blame for the observers' losses.
- Pyxis tried to free Elias indirectly with Polymorph, then pleaded with Elias in Celestial and continued attacking the captors.
- Widget committed because the mystery matters to Pyxis and because he does not abandon friends.
- Widget modified his jaguar construct's saddle to fit the griffon temporarily, then used rocket boots to fly upward and resisted a mist-vision of abandonment.
- Widget recognized that freeing Elias may require magic he lacks, teleported a gloomweaver to himself, and tried a brief negotiation before Cringer attacked.
- Soren agreed to help despite not currently wanting to leave Barovia, especially if the observer may hurt people or help his friends.
- Soren flew upward under his own wings, panicked in the mist, followed Pyxis and the griffon, and arrived exhausted before preparing scroll magic.
- Soren judged that Elias may not want to fight and asked how to free him; he later cast an unspecified heartening spell for the group.
- Inge joined for knowledge and understanding, whether or not escape is possible.
- Inge flew by arcane manipulation and failed or was overcome by a mist-vision of guilt about leaving her family; she arrived on the platform sobbing.
- Inge compartmentalized her distress, slowed the initial enemies, resisted a Hypnotic Pattern with help from Marcel's music, resisted Suffering Echoes, and damaged or muddled a whisperer's mind.
